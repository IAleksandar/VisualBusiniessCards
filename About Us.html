About Us

We are a Young and passionate Team, working from Macedonia to offer feasible and affordable solutions to all artists around the word. InRoomPreview is a Startup created by Vasil Stojchev in 2016. After seeing the difficulty of buying artworks without being able to visualize them at home, he decided to fix this problem by creating a virtualization App. Since then, the company has been constantly growing and expands now to the European market.
